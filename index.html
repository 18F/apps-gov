What is a "product"?

A product on Apps.Gov is a cloud-hosted technology or service that utilizes centralized servers to store and serve data to their users.

This allows the product to be more cost effective and easy to maintain as well as accessible from multiple devices and locations. Products are typically categorized as a Software as a Service (SaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), or any other X as a Service.

How are products selected?

Information on the product, company, existing contract vehicles, and government approvals is listed for each product on the products page. Companies are encouraged to submit or update their product information via the open source GitHub repository.
